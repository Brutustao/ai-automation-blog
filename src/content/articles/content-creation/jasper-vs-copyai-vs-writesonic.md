AI writing tools can dramatically speed up content production, but which one is
right for your marketing team? Here's how Jasper, Copy.ai, and Writesonic stack
up.

## Overview

| Feature | Jasper | Copy.ai | Writesonic |
|---------|--------|---------|------------|
| Starting Price | $49/mo | $36/mo | $19/mo |
| Free Trial | 7 days | 7 days | Free tier |
| Word Limit | Unlimited (Starter) | Unlimited | Up to 6,250/mo free |
| Brand Voice | Yes | Yes | Limited |
| SEO Features | Built-in | External only | Built-in |
| Languages | 30+ | 95+ | 25+ |

## Jasper: Best for Brand Voice Consistency

Jasper excels at maintaining consistent brand voice across content. Its
Brand Voice feature lets you train the AI on your existing copy. Best for
teams that produce high volumes of on-brand content.

**Best for:** Mid-to-large marketing teams
**Affiliate link:** [Try Jasper →](https://www.jasper.ai/)

## Copy.ai: Best for Workflow Automation

Copy.ai's strength is workflow automation. You can create automated marketing
workflows that generate content at scale. With support for 95+ languages, it's
the best choice for international teams.

**Best for:** International marketing teams
**Affiliate link:** [Try Copy.ai →](https://www.copy.ai/)

## Writesonic: Best Value

Writesonic offers the most affordable entry point with a usable free tier. Its
built-in SEO tools (including Article Writer 5.0) make it a strong choice for
content marketers on a budget.

**Best for:** Solopreneurs and small teams
**Affiliate link:** [Try Writesonic →](https://writesonic.com/)

## Verdict

- **Choose Jasper** if brand voice consistency is your top priority
- **Choose Copy.ai** if you need multi-language content at scale
- **Choose Writesonic** if you're on a tight budget or just starting out
